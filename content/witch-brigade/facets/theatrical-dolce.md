# Theatrical Dolce

> A theater dancer that flourishes behind the scenes.
>
> A facet that seduces their opponents with sensual dances.
>
> As a flanker, they excel at long-distance battle.

## Etymology

*Dolce* is Italian for *sweet*.[^1]
